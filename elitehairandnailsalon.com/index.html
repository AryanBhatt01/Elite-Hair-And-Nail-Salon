OUR SERVICES

Nail Technician

Here at Elite Hair & Nail Salon, We want to ensure fantastic results every time, which is why we choose the best nail products and latest techniques in our salon. We provide a full range of nail treatments to suit your needs and budget, including manicures, nail polishing, gel nails, pedicures. Please see our prices page for a full list of options.

Hair

From roots to tips, every hair strand is covered with a deep, rich, solid color filled with shine. To enhance your natural color, for grey coverage or to go darker or lighter, Our stylists can craft a personalized custom color to fit your needs.

Eyelash Extensions

Eyelash extensions are used to enhance the length, curliness, fullness, and thickness of natural eyelashes. The extensions may be made from several materials including mink or synthetic materials.

Facial and Skincare

Elite Salon is a full service Salon offering Facial and Skincare. Certified Specialists are here to customize your skin’s needs using the quality brands, products and techniques for your facials.
